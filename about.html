About PRINCE PUSHA

Founder of ROYVLTY EMPIRE, Music Engineer, and Artist Developer based in Sarasota, FL. I've been doing music since I was young and it's more than a career — it's a calling.

One pivotal moment in my journey: I mixed a song for someone not knowing who he was — he ended up being a major figure. That track caught attention and earned props from Timbaland. Since then, I've been shaping records with precision and passion.

ROYVLTY EMPIRE was built because I know the struggle of being an independent artist — not just hungry for money, but for knowledge. I swore to create something for artists to get the tools I never had.

With 9 years in the game and fully self-taught, I’ve worked with artists like Soulja Skip, Geno Mays, BIGG KC, Chaz OG, Ben J, King SRQ, BLOWIN BANDZ, 4MG JAYY DUBB, and more. I continue to collaborate with B-list labels and build with artists from the ground up.

What makes me different? I’m not here to gatekeep. I’m for the artist. I’m for the people. If you're serious about your craft, let's build together.
